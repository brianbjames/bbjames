CandleKing is a candlestick reference tool for traders and investors for the iPhone and iPod touch. It includes 50 candlestick patterns with descriptions. It is perfect for investors, day traders, finance students, and anyone else looking to improve entries and exits in the stock market, FX market, or any other market charted with candles.
